Bio

Hello again, Robert here! Let me tell you a little bit about myself. Born in '95, I grew up in the city of Anahiem, California. At the age of 13 years old in2008, I began my journey into game development and general programming.

I started making little 2D games in a game engine calledGame Maker 8. After a few of years of making little games in Game Maker, by2013I made the switch over toUnity. This would become my main game engine. Also came along with learning more programming languages. This is where I learned C-Sharp (C#) and Object-Oriented Programming (OOP).

I had a fantastic experience learning about developing a game. After many years of game development, in2019I wanted to learning about web development. I took many online bootcamps and that summer I signed up to go to theCollege of Western Idaho. Where I learned to hone in my craft has a software developer.

Today, and onwards I am a student to all technologies. As it still grows and improves, I want to be near first in line to the next technologies.
